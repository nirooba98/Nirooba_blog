What is Mad Libs?

Mad Libs is a fun game in which some words are removed and replaced with blank lines. Then, a player requests the other player to name a few adjectives, nouns, verbs, colors, numbers, etc. to fill in the blank spaces. Now you have a story with some funny and unrelatable words, which makes it hysterical.
